My name is Ryan Lewis and I am a high end Emmy Award winning 3D and web Generalist.

I have been in the industry for over 15 years. My knowledge extends from storyboarding any project to full completion and getting it fully ready for any media needed. I have managing experience for over 10 years and hands-on experience for over 15 years. My expertise range from conceptual design, digital design, 3D animation, directing, green screens and blue screens, visual effects, CG and live, web design, web build to compositing and completing the project for any media.


I am a person who uses everything and anything to his advantage, whether it be a picture of something I find or something I have to create, or a visual effect to complete a shot.


I am always widening my range and live to learn as much as I can. I am a person who does not have to be told to go work, I will always take the initiative and run with it. I have never missed a deadline and I do not intend to ever miss one.


I am fully licensed. and have an amazing team I work with on larger projects.


I am driven by sheer passion and love of the game and a belief that nothing is impossible!
